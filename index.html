Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about our development process, community playtests and platform support.

Sign up to our playtest list via the "Join playtest list" button in the hero. We run scheduled community playtests and occasional closed sessions — we’ll email participants with platform, build and timing details.

We primarily target PC (Steam) and selected console platforms. Release platforms are chosen per‑title based on scope and community demand; details are announced on each project's page.

Feedback from playtests is collected via survey links and in‑session notes. For bug reports or post-release feedback, email hello@seabloomgames.au with a clear description, platform, and steps to reproduce; we prioritise fixes based on severity and player impact.
